Make a Difference in the World of Art

At the National Gallery of Historic Art, our mission to preserve and celebrate historic artistic achievements relies on the support and generosity of individuals, businesses, and communities. Your contributions enable us to continue offering inspiring exhibitions, educational programs, and special events that bring art and history to life for all.

How You Can Support Us

  1. Donate: Your financial contributions help us maintain our galleries, acquire new artworks, and support ongoing conservation efforts. Every gift, large or small, makes a significant impact. Consider making a one-time donation or setting up a recurring gift to support our mission.
  2. Become a Member: Join our community of art lovers and history enthusiasts by becoming a member. Membership offers exclusive benefits such as early access to exhibitions, special invitations to member-only events, and discounts on programs and merchandise.
